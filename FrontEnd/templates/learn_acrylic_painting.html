FAQ

What are some essential materials needed for acrylic painting?

Essential materials for acrylic painting include acrylic paints, brushes of various sizes and shapes, canvas or painting surface, palette, palette knife, water container, and a protective coat such as varnish.

How do you thin acrylic paint?

Acrylic paint can be thinned with water or acrylic mediums such as flow improver, glazing medium, or retarder to achieve different consistencies and effects.

How do you clean acrylic painting brushes?

To clean acrylic painting brushes, rinse them thoroughly with water immediately after use. Use mild soap and water for deeper cleaning if necessary. Avoid letting acrylic paint dry on the brushes as it can be difficult to remove.

Can you use acrylic paint on canvas?

Yes, acrylic paint can be used on canvas. Canvas is a common painting surface for acrylics due to its durability and versatility. It is important to prime the canvas before painting to create a suitable surface for acrylic paints.
